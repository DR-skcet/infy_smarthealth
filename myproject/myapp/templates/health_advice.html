What is a healthy heart rate?
A normal resting heart rate for adults is between 60 and 100 beats per minute.

To lower blood pressure, reduce salt intake, maintain a healthy weight, and exercise regularly.
How many calories should I consume daily?
Daily calorie needs vary, but an average adult requires about 2000-2500 calories per day.

Aim for at least 10,000 steps per day for optimal health benefits.
